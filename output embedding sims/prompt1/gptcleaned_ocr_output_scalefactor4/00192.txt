1. Early Influences

The following theorists significantly influenced their area. The influence of their assumptions can be seen reflected in the field of communication.

1.1 Leon Festinger (1919-1989)

Leon Festinger studied under Kurt Lewin, a substantial influence in social psychology and a pioneer in the field of group dynamics. Festinger is best known for his Theory of Cognitive Dissonance, which explains how an imbalance among cognitions can affect an individual. Festinger's observations regarding cognitive dissonance theory were highly influential in the emerging field of social psychology.
