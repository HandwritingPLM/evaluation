THE CULTURE INDUSTRY: ENLIGHTENMENT AS MASS DECEPTION

Commercialization of culture in the United States had gone far beyond anything seen in Europe. Further, the prevalence of mass deception in the United States was something with which Europe could not compare. The closest thing in Europe was the concept of the culture industry.

Jager and Adorno were later both involved in the Fladio Filesearch Project in the field of culture and consumerism. At 18, Jager saw this research as crucial in distinguishing propaganda. Horkheimer and Adorno did not agree on the value of empirical studies, and the purpose of the Project was unclear.
