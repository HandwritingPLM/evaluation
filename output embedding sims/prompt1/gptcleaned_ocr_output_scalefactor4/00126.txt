Chapter 1

Uncertainty Reduction

Advances in Interpersonal Communication: Charles Berger, Richard Calabrese and Kevin Uncertainty Theorists

Since the mid-twentieth century, the concern for Uncertainty Reduction theory (URT) has significantly influenced research. The theory emerged as a response to the need to reduce uncertainty in social interactions and has been utilized by social psychologists in various research studies. This chapter provides a brief overview of the history of interpersonal communication.
