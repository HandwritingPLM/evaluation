BEDS AND GRADATIONS

The reading at each test point should be entered in the table. The table should include a record of each test point as indicated by the GP spaces. The recorded results should be clear and readable.

Always check the patient's examination requirements and communicate clearly.

Please prepare each patient for examination by explaining the procedure fully and obtaining consent. Always ensure the patient is comfortable and in a suitable position for the examination.

Remember to record any abnormal findings or concerns and report them immediately to the appropriate healthcare provider.

Thank you.
