Harold Lasswell (1902-1978) was a prominent scholar in the area of propaganda, conducting both quantitative and qualitative analyses of propaganda, understanding and discovering the effect of propaganda on the mass audience (Rogers, 1994). Lasswell is credited with developing the mass communication procedure of content analysis (Rogers, 1994).
