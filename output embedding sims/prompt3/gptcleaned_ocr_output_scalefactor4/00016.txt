1 Early Influences

1.1 Kurt Lewin (1890-1947)

After graduating initially in Physics from the University of Fribourg, Lewin continued his studies at the University of Berlin. He worked at the Psychological Institute, University of Berlin, before moving to the United States in 1933 and joining the staff at the University of Iowa (Rogers, 1994).

1.2 Fritz Heider (1896-1988)

Heider was born in Vienna, Austria. He obtained his Ph.D. in Psychology from the University of Berlin in 1926. He later moved to the United States and became a faculty member at the University of Kansas (Rogers, 1994).
