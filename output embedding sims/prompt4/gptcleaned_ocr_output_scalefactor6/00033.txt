Bees a species in a specialized group.
As in many other animal groups in the animal kingdom, bees can fly and is an essential pollinator in the ecosystem.
Furthermore, bees have a significant role in the horticulture industry.
They are known to be very hardworking. Bees are also known for their organized behavior when building their hives.
However, bees can pose a danger to humans when they feel threatened.
Please be careful when dealing with bees to avoid getting stung.
